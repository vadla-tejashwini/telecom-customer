Customer Churn Nalysis In Telecom Industry
High-Value Customer Segment
A small percentage of customers contribute to a large portion of total revenue.
These customers have high CLV, low churn risk, and frequent usage.
Churn vs CLV Relationship
Customers with low CLV often show higher churn risk.
Identifying this helps target retention strategies early.
Service Usage Impact
Customers using multiple services/products tend to have higher CLV.
Cross-selling increases lifetime value.
Revenue Concentration
Majority of revenue comes from top-tier customers.
Losing these customers can significantly impact business performance.
Customer Lifecycle Trends
CLV grows over time with consistent engagement and satisfaction.
Early-stage customers need nurturing to increase long-term value.
